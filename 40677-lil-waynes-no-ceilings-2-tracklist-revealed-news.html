Lil Wayne's &quot;No Ceilings 2&quot; Tracklist Revealed

After hitting us with a trailer to increase the anticipation and excitement for No Ceilings 2 last night, Weezy F returns this afternoon with a tracklist, so we know it's real.

The highly anticipated mixtape will serve as a follow-up to the much-beloved first No Ceilings, and thus it's got quite a reputation to live up too. The tracklist is stacked though, so let's hope Weezy didn't spare quality for quantity.

Check out the tracklist below. Features come from Mannie Fresh, Future, Yo Gotti, Jae Millz, King Los, Curren$y (!), and more. Be sure to check back with us later for the download.
