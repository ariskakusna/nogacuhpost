Jon Moxley Inspired By Iconic DC Character for Wrestle Kingdom 18 Gear

Jon Moxley remains one of the top stars in AEW and he is currently competing in a massive match at Wrestle Kingdom 18 for the IGWP Global Heavyweight Title. Moxley came out looking like an absolute boss and it appears his gear was inspired by a famous DC character.

Wrestle Kingdom 18 has been going strong so far as it featured quite few terrific surprises and numerous title changes over the course of the past few hours. Nevertheless, one of the show’s biggest draws is going on right now.

Jon Moxley, Will Ospreay, and David Finlay are squaring off against each other for the IGWP Global Heavyweight Title in what is expected to be a solid bout. During Moxley’s entrance, he came out looking for vengeance.

In case you didn’t know, Jon Moxley’s ring gear was inspired by the DC character the Red Hood, who used to work with Batman when he was once Robin. Naturally, fans loved every moment of it.

We will have to wait and see who will end up becoming the first IGWP Global Heavyweight Champion, as many fans feel Jon Moxley should win the title and etch his name in history.
