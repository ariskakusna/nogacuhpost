GloRilla's New &quot;Lick Or Sum&quot; Single Samples &quot;Slob On My Nob&quot;: Stream

In 2022, Memphis-based rap diva GloRilla made her mark on the world thanks to the far-reaching success of "F.N.F. (Let's Go)" and its remix. On top of that, she also delivered her debut EP, Anyways, Life's Great... which saw her link up with Cardi B for another fan favourite, "Tomorrow 2." So far, this year has seen her showing out with equal intensity. We've heard her deliver collaborations like "On Wat U On" with Moneybagg Yo and "Internet Trolls" alongside Hitkidd.

On Tuesday (May 23), Big Glo stood out from the New Music Friday crowd with her latest single. "Lick Or Sum" came along with a twerk-filled music video this afternoon. It samples 2000's "Slob On My Nob" from Tear Da Club Up Thugs, Three 6 Mafia, and Project Pat. Filled with NSFW lyrics, the nearly two-and-a-half-minute-long track is sure to be heavily quoted on social media over the coming weeks.

GloRilla and Friends Let Loose in the "Lick Or Sum" Visual

"Lick on my cl*t, make this p*ssy cream / Do this motherf*cker how you do the Russian Creams," the 23-year-old begins her first verse. "Smokin' on this pack, you know the p*ssy fat / Lookin' like he fiendin', got his nose all in my crack, GloRilla continues, only becoming more salacious as she goes along. While she handles the rhyming by herself, the new video finds her linking up with plenty of friends, letting loose and having fun while dancing along to her soon-to-be summer anthem.
