The Undertaker Cant Wait To Celebrate 30 Years Of WWE RAW

The Undertaker walked into a WWE ring for the first time 33 years ago. The Deadman main evented the very first edition of RAW as well. Tomorrow night, the icon returns to the red brand.

The Undertaker is set to appear on the 30th anniversary edition of RAW tomorrow night in Philadelphia. The Phenom will join fellow Hall of Famers and legends of the business including Shawn Michaels, Triple H, and Kurt Angle.

The Undertaker took to Twitter ahead of the momentous occasion and shared his thoughts. The real-life Mark Calaway noted he can’t wait to celebrate 30 years of the red brand.

From being in the main event of the very first episode and everything that followed, it’s been a wild three decades!

Can’t wait to celebrate 30 years of #WWERaw! #RawisXXX

Blue Meanie is open to making an appearance on the anniversary show as well. The ECW original made a brief return to WWE in 2005. He recently revealed he had informed WWE that he’d be available to appear on the show if they wanted him.

The 30th-anniversary show will serve as the go-home edition for the upcoming Royal Rumble premium live event . You can check out the line-up for January 23, 2023, edition of RAW below:

  • The Usos (c) vs. The Judgment Day – RAW Tag Team Championship Match
  • Austin Theory (c) vs Bobby Lashley – United States Championship Match
  • Bayley vs Becky Lynch – Steel Cage Match
  • Trial of Sami Zayn
  • WWE legends will appear
