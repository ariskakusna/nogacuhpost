FAQs

Does Hairstory offer free shipping?

Yes, HairStory offers free standard shipping across all US states if your order totals $55. If your order is less than this amount, then standard shipping costs $8.95. If your order is part of the Refill Club, then shipping is also free.  

Does HairStory offer a student discount?

As of writing, HairStory does not have a student discount in place. If this changes, we’ll update this page to let you know. Students can still save on their orders by shopping the sale or using one of our HairStory promo codes. 

Does HairStory have a welcome offer?

Yes. Sign up to the HairStory newsletter to get 20% off your first order. You'll also be kept updated on the latest HairStory discount codes, promotions, and free hair styling advice. If you've shopped at Hairstory before, check our page for regular discount codes for 10% off selected products.

How often does HairStory have sales?

HairStory does have sales from time to time. Whilst there is no dedicated sales page on their website, you can always follow them on social media or via their newsletter for sales updates. We’ll also update this page with any sales or coupon codes so be sure to check back frequently.  

Does HairStory offer free returns?

Returns at HairStory can be made free of charge, however HairStory cannot refund shipping costs, only the cost of the product. Ensure you return your order within 60 days.

How to use your promo code at HairStory

  • Select your perfect HairStory code from Marie Claire. 
  • Continue to the HairStory website by clicking 'Get Discount'.
  • Then, add your products to your cart.
  • Click on 'Checkout' on the screen that appears.
  • On the left-hand side of the screen is a 'Gift card or discount code' box. 
  • Enter your promo code into the box.
