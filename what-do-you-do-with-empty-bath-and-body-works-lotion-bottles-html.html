What is the best way to dispose of lotion bottles?

Empty the contents of lotion bottles and other toiletry goods that have pumps, then discard the pump and recycle the container, if appropriate. For no-pump containers, just dump the contents into a trash bag, rescrew the lid to keep everything together, and throw it away.
