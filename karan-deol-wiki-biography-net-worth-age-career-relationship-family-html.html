Karan Deol, born on November 27, 1990, is an aspiring Indian actor and assistant director based in Mumbai, India. He belongs to the renowned Deol family and is the son of legendary actor Sunny Deol, who is also a prominent BJP leader. With a strong lineage in the film industry, Karan Deol has set his sights on making a name for himself in Bollywood. His debut film, “Pal Pal Dil Ke Paas,” is a family production, where he not only stars as the lead but also serves as an assistant director. The movie was primarily shot in scenic locations such as Manali and other hilly regions of Himachal Pradesh.
