Clout Chaser: Bobriskys Ex-PA Oyes Friend Backtracks After Claiming She Died, Fans React

  • A report surfaced recently that Bobrisky’s former Ivorian personal assistant, Oye Kyme, had passed on
  • The disturbing news about the Ivorian lady was announced by one of her friends on social media
  • The news of Oye’s death is coming just days after she contracted COVID-19 and asked fans to pray for her recovery

Nigerian crossdresser Bobrisky’s controversial former personal assistant, Oye Kyme, was reported to have passed on.

On October 28, 2021, the news made the rounds online that the young Ivorian lady is no more. One of Oye’s friends, Blessing Oyekan, took to social media to break the sad news.

This came just days after the crossdresser’s former PA revealed online that she had tested positive for COVID-19.

The young lady posted a photo of herself with Oye and accompanied it with a heart-wrenching note where she mourned the demise of her dear friend.
