Jennifer Garner Net Worth

Jennifer Garner is a Golden Globe-winning actor who was born on April 17, 1972 in Houston, TX. She is best known for her role as CIA secret agent Sydney Bristow on the thriller series Alias, as well as her role as Jenna Rink in 13 Going on 30.

About

Golden Globe-winning actor who became known for portraying CIA secret agent Sydney Bristow on the thriller series Alias. She starred as Jenna Rink in 13 Going on 30. 

Before Fame

She spent the majority of her childhood practicing ballet after having her first lesson when she was three. She joined the Pi Beta Phi sorority in college. 

Trivia

She starred as the title character in the 2005 film Elektra. She played Vanessa Loring in 2007's Juno.  

Family Life

In 2005, she married Ben Affleck. Then in 2015, the couple announced they were separating. She and Affleck have two daughters, Violet and Seraphina, and a son, Samuel. She was married to actor Scott Foley from 2000 to 2004. 

Associated With

She starred with Kevin Spacey in the film Nine Lives.
