FAQs

Does Mytheresa offer a first-order discount code?

Yes, new shoppers at Mytheresa can get 10% off first orders of $700 or more. You can also sign up to the Mytheresa newsletter for promotions and sales updates. 

How can I get free shipping at Mytheresa?

When you sign up for text updates at Mytheresa, you will get free shipping on your next order. Alternatively, any order over $400 will qualify for free US shipping. If your order is less than this, standard shipping costs $25.  

Does Mytheresa have a student discount?

Unfortunately, Mytheresa does not offer any student discount at this time. If you’re a student, you can still save on your purchase by shopping in the ‘Sale’ section or using one of our Mytheresa promo codes. If Mytheresa adds a student discount in the future, we’ll let you know right here. 

Does Mytheresa have any sales?

Yes, simply go to the Mytheresa ‘Sale’ page on the brand’s website. You can save on designer brands all year round, with previous discounts of up to 50% off. You can usually save hundreds of dollars on designers such as Burberry, Versace, Valentino, and Brunello Cucinelli.  

What is the Mytheresa returns policy?

Returns can be made to Mytheresa within 30 days of receipt for most items with your pre-paid returns label. Your items should be returned in original condition – unworn, unwashed, and unaltered. You should also have any tags still attached and include original shoeboxes, jewelry boxes, and dust bags. Any customized items, LIFE items, or ‘Final Sale’ items cannot be returned or exchanged.  

 Hints & Tips 

Newsletter Promotions: Sign up for the Mytheresa newsletter to save on your next designer purchase. You’ll be sent the latest trends, access to private sales, and the newest products straight to your inbox. To sign up, go to the Mytheresa website, scroll to the footer, and type your email address. 

Shop holiday sales: Save on high-end fashion by shopping during seasonal periods, such as holidays. Mytheresa usually has Christmas and Black Friday sales, with great savings. You can also shop for popular dates for fashion sales, such as the 4th of July, Mother’s Day, or New Year. We’ve previously seen discounts of 50-70% off with these sales. 

Text discounts: Sign up for Mytheresa texts to get free shipping on your next order. You’ll also be sent exclusive products and be the first to hear about new clothing items. Go to the Mytheresa website, scroll to the footer, and type your phone number into the sign-up box. 

Stay social: Follow Mytheresa on social for the latest exclusive products and clothing as they go live. You can also get style inspirations and see behind the scenes of the brand – like its recent trip to Shanghai for a collaboration.
