Who is Sergio Ramos wife, Pilar Rubio?

Sergio Ramos is considered one of the best defenders in the history of football, and has earned the title through captaining both his club and country to various trophies. The 35-year old Spaniard recently left Real Madrid after 16 years of service, and now sports the badge of Paris Saint-Germain.

Who is Sergio Ramos’ wife?

Spanish Reporter and TV presenter Pilar Rubio Fernández is the wife of footballer Sergio Ramos. Born in Madrid, Pilar Rubio is a self-made Spanish reporter and TV presenter. She initially began studying economics, but did not follow through till the degree.

Pilar Rubio has modelled in many men’s magazines, and has appeared in many movies such as Isi and Disi, Afterparty and Carlitos and the chance of a lifetime. Pilar Rubio has also appeared in commercials for Canal+, Hyundai and in programs like The Price is Right, Louisiana azotea de Wyoming. She was also in the comedy program Louisiana ventana indiscreta.

She also won the best reporter award with Premio Joven 2007. Pilar was also voted sexiest woman in the world by the Spanish edition of the FHM magazine. Pilar also has two siblings, a brother; Alberto Rubio and a sister; Esther Rubio.

How old is Pilar Rubio?

Born on March 17, 1978, Pilar Rubio is a Pisces. She is 43 years old and is 5 feet 4.5 inches tall. Pilar Rubio is very active on instagram, and has garnered a following of 6.2 million.

Training and travel posts are a common find on her page, with the occasional ad. She loves posing with her husband and 4 kids as well.

Pilar is often seen in the stands when her husband Sergio is playing.

How did Pilar Rubio and Sergio Ramos meet?

Ramos and Rubio met for the first time when Rubio was working as a TV presenter on the ‘Se lo que hicisteis’ set and interviewed the football sensation. Sergio Ramos admits that he dreamed of the reporter many times, before finally contacting her via SMS, and breaking the ice.

The couple started dating in 2012 when Ramos was 26 and Rubio was 34. The two were confirmed to be dating during the 2013 ballon d’or ceremony which they attended together.

The two got engaged in July of 2018, and tied the knot in June of 2019. The wedding took place in Ramos’ hometown of Seville .

Does Sergio Ramos have children?

Ramos and Rubio announced in November of 2013 that they were expecting a child, and on May 6, 2014 Sergio Jr. was born. In May of 2015 they said that they were expecting their second child, and Rubio gave birth to Marco on November 14 ,2015.

Their third child goes by the name of Alejandro, born on 25 March 2018.

On July 26,2020, Ramos and Rubio announced the birth of their fourth child, Maximo.

The family are often pictured working out at the gym together. They also show Ramos’s musical talent, after a video surfaced of Sergio teaching Sergio Jr. to play the Piano.
