Brian Tee Net Worth

Brian Tee is a Japanese-Korean actor born on March 15, 1977 in Okinawa, Japan. He is best known for his roles as The Drift King in The Fast and the Furious: Tokyo Drift and Fast & Furious 7. He has also had roles in Zoey 101, Grimm, and most recently, the NBC drama Chicago Med as Dr. Ethan Choi.

About

Japanese-Korean actor most famous for his role as The Drift King in 2006's The Fast and the Furious: Tokyo Drift and 2015's Fast & Furious 7. He landed the role of Dr. Ethan Choi in the NBC drama Chicago Med and has also appeared in Zoey 101 and Grimm.

Before Fame

He moved from Okinawa, Japan to Hacienda Heights, California at the age of 2. He was the captain of the football team at Glen A. Wilson High School and earned a degree in Theatre from the University of California, Berkeley.

Trivia

He appeared as himself in the music video for "Dance Like Michael Jackson" by the Los Angeles-based hip-hop quartet Far East Movement in 2008. He had a role in the 2015 film Jurassic World.

Family Life

He married Mirelly Taylor, known for her role on the series Lost. In 2015 they welcomed a daughter named Madelyn.

Associated With

He appeared alongside Hugh Jackman in 2013's The Wolverine.
