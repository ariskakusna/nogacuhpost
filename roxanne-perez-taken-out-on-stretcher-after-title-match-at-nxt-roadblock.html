Roxanne Perez Taken Out On Stretcher After Title Match At NXT Roadblock

Meiko Satomura took Roxanne Perez to the limit in the main event of NXT Roadblock tonight. The prodigy retained her NXT Women’s Championship against the Japanese legend, but at the cost of her physical wellbeing.

Roxanne Perez successfully defended her title against the former NXT Women’s UK Champion in the main event of NXT Roadblock tonight. The Japanese legend delivered some stuff kicks on Roxanne during their highly-physical matchup.

Satomura raised Roxanne’s hand following her win over her. Unfortunately, the beating took its toll on the champion as she collapsed from exhaustion on the map. The referee noticed that Roxanne didn’t get up and called for help as Satmoura looked on in disbelief.

Booker T left the commentary to check on her former star pupil. Medics brought a stretcher to the ring to help escort Perez out of the building. Shawn Michaels came out to check on the NXT Women’s Champion as well.

The NXT Universe cheered on Roxanne Perez as the medics escorted her to the ambulance. NXT Roadblock ended with medics placing the stretcher inside the ambulance.
