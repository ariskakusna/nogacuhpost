Xolo Mariduea Wiki, Biography, Net Worth, Age, Career, Relationship,Family

 

Xolo Maridueña, a talented actor born on June 9, 2001, in America, has captured the hearts of many with his roles as Victor Graham in the TV series Parenthood and Miguel Diaz in Netflix’s Cobra Kai.

Xolo Maridueña’s Parents:

Curious about the family behind the actor? Xolo Maridueña’s parents are an integral part of his life story. Residing in Los Angeles, Xolo was born to Mr. Omar G. Ramirez, a music composer, and Mrs. Carmelita Ramirez Sanchez, a radio presenter. Alongside his parents, Xolo has four siblings: Oshun and Talia, his sisters, and a younger brother whose name adds to the family’s rich bonds.

Early Life and Education:

Raised in an upper-middle-class household in Los Angeles, California, Xolo Maridueña’s upbringing played a pivotal role in shaping his artistic journey. He proudly identifies as an American and holds Christian values close to his heart. Xolo’s educational path led him to Cathedral High School in California, where he received his diploma before delving into the world of acting. Fuelled by his family’s artistic background, Xolo discovered his passion for singing and acting from a young age. This enthusiasm led him to the Young Actors Space in California and even the New York Film Institute in 2020.

Career Highlights:

Remarkably, Xolo Maridueña’s acting career took flight at the tender age of 9 when he embarked on his professional journey with roles in print and television commercials. His breakthrough came in 2012 when he secured a significant role in the acclaimed series “Parenthood.” In subsequent years, Xolo’s talent landed him guest appearances on various television shows, including Mack & Moxy and Rush Hour. He also took on the role of “Manny” in the sitcom “Dealin’ with Idiots” and starred in the television film “Furst Born.” Beyond these endeavors, Xolo lent his voice to characters in the animated series “Victor and Valentino” and the science fiction series “Cleopatra in Space.”

Relationships and Personal Life:

In matters of the heart, Xolo Maridueña has been romantically linked with Hannah Kepple, who also appeared in Cobra Kai. Before that, he was in a relationship with Jayka Noelle.

Awards and Achievements:

Xolo Maridueña’s exceptional performances have not gone unnoticed. In 2014, he received the Imagen Award for Outstanding Youth Ensemble in a TV Series and the Best Young Actor/Television award. Moreover, he clinched the Young Artist Award for Outstanding Performance by a Supporting Young Actor in the same year. His talent continued to shine, earning him nominations for the “Young Artist Award” and recognition at the “Imagen Foundation Awards” and the “Teen Choice Award.”

Net Worth:

As of 2023, Xolo Maridueña’s net worth stands at an impressive $2.5 million. His earnings predominantly come from his roles in television and film projects, complemented by a notable income from live streaming activities.

Nationality and Ancestry:
