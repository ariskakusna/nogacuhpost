The Saturdays show their lighter side for Comic Relief

    The Saturdays showed the lighter way to celebrate Red Nose Day by battling it out with a chocolate challenge. The reality show girls have teamed up with Maltesers to try and raise over £1million for Red Nose Day by carrying out a string of cheeky challenges. Rochelle, Frankie, Una, Vanessa and Mollie let their competitive streak get the better of them as they battled it out to be the last girl standing with a Malteser balanced on her tongue whilst singing at the same time.

    PHOTO TO ENLARGE

    While the girl group looked like they were having a ball clowning around on the photoshoot wearing comedy noses, there was a serious message behind their fun and games. Every pack of sweets sold, Maltesers will donate 5p towards the Comic Relief charity. In between giggles blonde winner Mollie King said: "Everyone needs to let their lighter side out once in a while.

    "We had so much fun trying out all the cheeky challenges – the girls all tease me for being a bit competitive but my desire to win paid off when I was victorious! You don't have to do a big challenge to get involved, just get a packet of Maltesers and try them with your friends and family to try and help us smash the £1million target."
