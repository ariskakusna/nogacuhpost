From Beyoncé, Taylor Swift to Madonna, here are the other wealthy female singers

Taylor Swift came second on the list with a net worth of $740 million (N340bn). The award-winning singer's wealth comes from her music, concerts and tours.

Madonna is the third richest female singer with a net worth of $580 million (N266bn).

According to BolaVIP, the star is followed by Beyoncé Knowles who is worth $540 million (N248bn).
