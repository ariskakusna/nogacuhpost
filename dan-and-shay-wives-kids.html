Who are the wives of Dan and Shay?

DAN SMYERS and Shay Mooney are famously known as the country music duo Dan + Shay.

While the former is married to Abby Law, the latter is wedded to Hannah Billingsley; both weddings were featured in their Speechless music video.

Who is Dan Smyers' wife, Abby Law?

While Dan Smyers is a popular country singer, his wife Abby Law is a marketing professional and animal rescue advocate.

Throughout her career, Abby has been an ambassador for the brands Diff Eyewear, Hello Fresh, Fab Fit Fun, and The Dry House Nashville.

She and Dan have worked together with the animal rescue organizations Wags & Walks Rescue Nashville and the International Fund for Animal Welfare.

In 2013, the future Grammy Award winner met Abby at a Nashville animal rescue center.

On November 18, 2016, the couple announced their engagement.

Dan and Abby became betrothed after the singer proposed to the latter while on vacation in Waikiki, Hawaii.

"We were going to try and keep it super small but we have so many people that we love and care about and we both have decent-sized families," Abby told People.

Regarding her nuptials with Dan, she shared: "We’re very much looking forward to being husband and wife because our life has been really wonderful together."

On May 13, 2017, they tied the knot in a Nashville wedding ceremony.

Who is Shay Mooney's wife, Hannah Billingsley?

While Dan + Shay are each other's musical other halves, the latter is married to Hannah Billingsley.

Hannah is a former beauty pageant queen from Fayetteville, Arkansas.

In 2013, she won Miss Arkansas and represented her home state in that year's Miss USA competition.

Also hailing from Arkansas, Shay shared several mutual friends with Hannah.

For some time, they corresponded via Instagram and eventually met on March 11, 2015, at George’s Majestic Lounge.

Following their first encounter, The Voice coach asked Hannah to be his date to that year's ACM Awards in Dallas.

On August 9, 2016, the pair revealed they were engaged via Instagram.

It turns out, Shay and Hannah secretly wedded later that same day.

On October 20, 2017, the couple had a bigger wedding for their family and friends in their home state.

They have three children together; three sons named Asher, Ames, and Abram.

How did Dan + Shay meet?

The duo was formed after Shay met Dan at the latter's Nashville house party on December 7, 2012.

"I was living in a terrible house; it was the worst house of all time," Dan recalled to The Boot.

"Shay came over with a buddy. We started singing together, singing cover songs, and making stuff up on the spot.

"It was about four in the morning, and we were like, ‘We should write together.'

"At first, we were just two writers trying to get a publishing deal, to do whatever we had to do in this town."

Shay also remembered: "It was the magic in the room.

"It was cool to finally have that moment where, when we met and started writing, it was finally the country music we had tried to be writing separately."
