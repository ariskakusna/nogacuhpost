Nigerian lady becomes Father Christmas

Meanwhile, Legit.ng previously reported that a video of a Nigerian lady entertaining kids in Father Christmas costume had gone viral.

At the beginning of the video, she could be seen wearing the Father Christmas costume. She had to stuff the front of her jacket with clothes so her stomach looked protruding.

Satisfied with the way she was dressed up, she danced a little bit before sitting in the room where she was to welcome kids.

As it is expected of a Father Christmas, the lady handed different packages to the kids who came to see Santa Claus.
