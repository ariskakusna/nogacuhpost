PHOTOS: Tundun Abiola's Ex-Husband, Atama Attah Remarries to Presidential Aide Josephine Washima

A few months after his divorce from one of the daughter's of late Chief MKO, Tundun Abiola was finalized, popular socialite and businessman Atama Attah has remarried. He married Josephine Washima, President Jonathan's Special Adviser on Job Creation, on Saturday December 21.

Tundun, who is one of the most prominent Abiola daughters married Benue State-born Atama Attah at an opulent ceremony that held in London in May 2009 (Their wedding photos are online). They separated last year after only three years of marriage. Their divorce was finalized this year. The former couple have two children.

Atama has quickly moved on to Josephine.
