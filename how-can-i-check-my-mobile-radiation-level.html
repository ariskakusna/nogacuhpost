    Is 1.17 SAR value safe?

    The standard for safe SAR value is 1.6 W/kg. It means 1 Kg of your body can absorb about 1.6 Watts. Anything more than that can be harmful to your body tissues (aka your health). Therefore, when purchasing a mobile, you should check whether the SAR value is less than 1.6 W/Kg.
